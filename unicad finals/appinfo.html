“Unicad”

"Uni stands for university and CAD stands for academic."

The Unicad application features a scanner that allows users to scan QR codes on products to connect to the system and interact with the AI. This enables the product to activate the Digital Black Board using AI voice commands. This innovative feature enhances the user experience by providing a seamless and interactive way to access information and control the product.
